Bio

I am currently pursuing PhD in Computer Science at University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC) advised by Dr. Tim Oates (UMBC) and co-advised by Dr. David Chapman (U Miami). Prior to that I was a Research Assistant at Center for Cognitive Skill Enhancement (CCSE) at Independent University Bangladesh (IUB). I have received a Masters of Science in Computer Science from University of Maryland, Baltimore County in May 2023 and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from BRAC University in August 2017. My CV

My primary area of research is Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I am currently studying methods to impose strong shift invariance in CNNs, and methods to circumvent class imbalance in semantic segmentation models. I am broadly interested in investigating attention based and knowledge infused vision models in the application of self-driving vehicles and other low-level computer vision tasks.
