Bio

I am currently pursuing PhD in Computer Science at University of Maryland, Baltimore County advised by Dr. Tim Oates and co-advised by Dr. David Chapman. I am broadly interested in identifying and mitigating adversarial vulnerabilities in deep learning models.

My research has involved studying Backdoor Attacks in CNNs - curating attacks and building fast solutions to identify backdoors. I have also investigated how spatial context introduces vulnerabilities in fast object detectors like YOLO and proposed methods to train detectors robust to contextual adversarial patches.
